== Description ==

Enhance your email outreach with LetterPress, a robust plugin for seamless communication. Leverage its potent queue system to effortlessly dispatch any volume of emails to your registered users. With the LetterPress E-Mail Newsletter plugin, you can craft and dispatch countless campaigns interruption-free. Tailor your sending pace by configuring campaign intervals and emails per interval, optimizing for your server's capabilities.

Effortlessly synchronize subscriber lists with your WordPress users. Create limitless campaigns, run test broadcasts, and initiate campaigns at your convenience. LetterPress adheres to GDPR guidelines, enabling recipients to unsubscribe through the footer link.

Streamline subscriber management, sync, and delivery of boundless email campaigns. Experience the ease of this impactful E-Mail plugin for marketing and newsletters.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What types of campaigns are supported? =

Our plugin accommodates HTML format campaigns. You'll need to craft your E-Mail newsletter in HTML and insert it into the campaigns section.

= Can I preview emails before sending a campaign? =

Certainly! You have the option to send a test campaign to an E-Mail address you specify in the LetterPress settings page.

= Is there a browser version available for campaigns? =

Absolutely. Each campaign automatically includes a browser version URL at the top of the E-Mail. Recipients can easily access the browser version by clicking the link.

= How can recipients unsubscribe from emails? =

We've made unsubscribing easy. An individualized unsubscribe URL is included in the receiver's E-Mail footer. By clicking on it, they can instantly opt out from further communications.
